  • Gurgling fixtures or toilets

Repair Options Depend on the Condition of the Line

We do not treat every sewer problem the same. A line with grease buildup needs a different solution than a collapsed pipe. Camera findings, pipe material, depth, access and the location of the defect all affect the recommended approach.

ConditionPossible ApproachWhy Diagnosis Matters
Buildup or debrisDrain cleaning or hydro jettingConfirms the pipe is structurally sound before aggressive cleaning.
Roots at jointsRoot cutting, jetting and targeted repairShows whether roots are the only issue or a symptom of broken pipe.
Crack or offsetSpot repair, lining or excavationLocates the defect and helps compare repair methods.
Collapse or severe bellyExcavation or replacementEstablishes depth, length and the extent of failure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know whether I need Sewer repair?

A camera inspection and evaluation of the symptoms are the most reliable ways to determine the correct service. Recurring or multi-fixture problems often require more than a basic drain clearing.

How much does Sewer repair cost in Howell?

Pricing depends on access, pipe depth, line length, material, condition and the repair method. We recommend diagnosing the line before quoting a final scope.

Will you show me what the camera finds?

Yes. Clear visual findings help homeowners understand the cause of the problem and compare appropriate options.

Do you serve areas outside Howell?

Yes. We serve Howell and communities throughout Livingston County, including Brighton, Hartland, Pinckney, Fowlerville and Genoa Township.
